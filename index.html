Our Team

Most start-up founders wear multiple hats and scramble around. However, Beez, Nast B, Kel, and Zach began by operating in their individual geniuses. They believe in structure and that iron sharpens iron.

Founded in 2020, Double M Studios' humble beginnings started in Olney, MD. While the world was on pause, they laid the foundation to the community we see today. By 2023, Double M Studios moved to its first commercial property located in Rockville, MD.
